Going to the Jam

I actually didn't know Jams existed before Robin introduced me to that unknown world. The trip to the jams is somehow mystic for me. It resets my head, gives me time to come down from the busyness of the day. I observe the people around me as they scramble towards their homes or are just starting with their day. Before arriving, I like walking a bit in the dark, as there is almost not a soul to be seen and then I enter this world where your eyes are no longer the main character and your ears start slowly seeing. People laugh and exchange their feelings as they try to harmonize sounds they never played before.
